Question 10.
The probability a red marble selected at random from a jar containing p red, q blue and r green marbles is _________
(1) \(\frac{q}{p+q+r}\)
(2) \(\frac{p}{p+q+r}\)
(3) \(\frac{p+q}{p+q+r}\)
(4) \(\frac{p+r}{p+q+r}\)
Answer:
(1) \(\frac{q}{p+q+r}\)
Hint:
Sample spaces = p + q + r
Let A be the event of getting red
n(A) = p
P(A) = \(\frac{q}{p+q+r}\)
