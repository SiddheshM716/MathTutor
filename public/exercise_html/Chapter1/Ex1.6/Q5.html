Question 5.
The range of the relation R = {(x, x2) a prime number less than 13} is ……………………
(1) {2, 3, 5, 7}
(2) {2, 3, 5, 7, 11}
(3) {4, 9, 25, 49, 121}
(4) {1, 4, 9, 25, 49, 121}
Answer:
(3) {4, 9, 25, 49, 121}
Hint:
Prime number less than 13 = {2, 3, 5, 7, 11}
Range (R) = {(x, x2)}
Range = {4, 9, 25, 49, 121} (square of x)
