Question 3.
If A = {1,2}, B = {1,2, 3, 4}, C = {5,6} and D = {5, 6, 7, 8} then state which of the following statement is true ……………….
(1) (A × C) ⊂ (B × D)
(2) (B × D) ⊂ (A × C)
(3) (A × B) ⊂ (A × D)
(4) (D × A) ⊂ (B × A)
Answer:
(1) (A × C) ⊂ (B × D)
Hint: n(A × B) = 2 × 4 = 8
(A × C) = 2 × 2 = 4
n(B × C) = 4 × 2 = 8
n(C × D) = 2 × 4 = 8
n(A × C) = 2 × 2 = 4
n(A × D) = 2 × 4 = 8
n(B × D) = 4 × 4 = 16
∴ (A × C) ⊂ (B × D)
