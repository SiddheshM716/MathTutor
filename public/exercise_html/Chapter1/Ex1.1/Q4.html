Question 4.
If A ={5, 6}, B = {4, 5, 6} , C = {5, 6, 7}, Show that A × A = (B × B) ∩ (C × C).
Solution:
A = {5,6}, B = {4, 5, 6},C = {5, 6, 7}
A × A = {(5, 5), (5, 6), (6, 5), (6, 6)} ……….. (1)
B × B = {(4, 4), (4, 5), (4, 6), (5, 4),
(5, 5), (5, 6), (6, 4), (6, 5), (6, 6)} …(2)
C × C = {(5, 5), (5, 6), (5, 7), (6, 5), (6, 6),
(6, 7), (7, 5), (7, 6), (7, 7)} …(3)
(B × B) ∩ (C × C) = {(5, 5), (5,6), (6, 5), (6,6)} …(4)
(1) = (4)
A × A = (B × B) ∩ (C × C)
It is proved.
