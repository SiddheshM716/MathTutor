Question 3.
If (x – 6) is the HCF of x2 – 2x – 24 and x2 – kx – 6 then the value of k is ……………..
(1) 3
(2) 5
(3) 6
(4) 8
Answer:
(2) 5
Hint.
HCF = x – 6
p(x) = x2 – 2x – 24
= (x – 6) (x + 4)
g(x) = x2 – kx – 6
∴ x – 6 is the common factor.
g (6) = 62 – k(6) – 6
= 36 – 6k – 6
= 30 – 6k
g(6) = 0
30 – 6k = 0
30 = 6k ⇒ k = \(\frac { 30 }{ 6 } \) = 5
The value of k = 5
