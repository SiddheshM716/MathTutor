Question 2.
A has ‘a’ rows and ‘a + 3 ’ columns. B has ‘6’ rows and ‘17 – b’ columns, and if both products AB and BA exist, find a, b?
Solution:
A has a rows, a + 3 columns.
B has b rows, 17 – b columns
If AB exists a × a + 3
b × 17 – b
a + 3 = 6 ⇒ a – 6 = -3 ………… (1)
If BA exists 6 × 17-6
a × a + 3
17 – 6 = a ⇒ a + 6 = 17 …………. (2)
(1) + (2) ⇒ 2a = 14 ⇒ a = 7
Substitute a = 7 in (1) ⇒ 7 – b = -3 ⇒ b = 10
a = 7, b = 10
