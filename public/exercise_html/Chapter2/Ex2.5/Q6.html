Question 6.
Find the middle term(s) of an A.P. 9, 15, 21, 27, …, 183.
Answer:
First term (a) = 9
Last term (l) = 183
Common difference (d) = 15 – 9 = 6
n = \(\frac { l-a }{ d } \) + 1
= \(\frac { 183-9 }{ 6 } \) + 1
= \(\frac { 174 }{ 6 } \) + 1
= 29 + 1
= 30
middle term = 15th term of
16th term
tn = a + (n – 1)d
t15 = 9 + 14(6)
= 9 + 84 = 93
t16 = 9 + 15(6)
= 9 + 90 = 99
The middle term is 93 or 99
