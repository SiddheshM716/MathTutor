Question 3.
Find the first term and common difference of the Arithmetic Progressions whose nth terms are given below
(i) tn = -3 + 2n
(ii) tn = 4 – 7n
Solution:
(i) a = t1 = -3 + 2(1) = -3 + 2 = -1
d = t2 – t1
Here t2 = -3 + 2(2) = -3 + 4 = 1
∴ d = t2 – t1 = 1 – (-1) = 2
(ii) a = t1 = 4 – 7(1) = 4 – 7 = -3
d = t2 – t1
Here t2 = 4 – 7(2) = 4 – 14 – 10
∴ d = t2 – t1 = 10 – (-3) = -7
