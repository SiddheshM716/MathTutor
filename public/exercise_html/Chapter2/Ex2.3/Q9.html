Question 9.
Find the remainder when 281 is divided by 17?
Answer:
281 ≡ x(mod 17)
240 × 240 × 21 ≡ x(mod 17)
(24)10 × (24)10 × 21 ≡ x(mod 17)
(16)10 × (16)10 × 21 ≡ x(mod 17)
(162)5 × (162)5 × 21 ≡ x(mod 17)
= 1 × 1 × 2 (mod 17)
[(16)2 = 256 = 1 (mod 17)]
= 2 (mod 17)
281 = 2(mod 17)
∴ x = 2
The remainder is 2
