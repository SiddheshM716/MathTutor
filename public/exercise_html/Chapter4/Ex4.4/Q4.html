Question 4.
PQ is a tangent drawn from a point P to a circle with centre O and QOR is a diameter of the circle such that ∠POR = 120° . Find ∠OPQ.
Solution:
∠POQ = 180° – 120° = 60°
In ∆OPQ, we know

∠POQ + ∠OQP + ∠OPQ = 180°
(Sum of the angles of a ∆ is 180°)
60° + 90° + ∠OPQ = 180°
∠OPQ = 180° – 150° = 30°
∠OPQ = 30°
