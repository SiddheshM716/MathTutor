Question 1.
The length of the tangent to a circle from a point P, which is 25 cm away from the centre is 24 cm. What is the radius of the circle?
Solution:
Let the radius AB be r. In the right ∆ ABO,
OB2 = OA2 + AB2
252 = 242 + r2

252 – 242 = r2
(25 + 24) (25 – 24) = r2
r = \(\sqrt { 49 }\) =7
Radius of the circle = 7 cm
