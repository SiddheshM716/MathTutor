Question 15.
In figure if PR is tangent to the circle at P and O is the centre of the circle, then ∠POQ is ………….

(1) 120°
(2) 100°
(3) 110°
(4) 90°
Answer:
(1) 120°
Hint.
Since PR is tangent of the circle.
∠QPR = 90°
∠OPQ = 90° – 60° = 30°
∠OQB = 30°
In ∆OPQ
∠P + ∠Q + ∠O = 180°
30 + 30° + ∠O = 180°
(OP and OQ are equal radius)
∠O = 180° – 60° = 120°
