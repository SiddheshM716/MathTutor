Question 3.
If ∆ABC is an isosceles triangle with ∠C = 90° and AC = 5 cm, then AB is ………
(1) 2.5 cm
(2) 5 cm
(3) 10 cm
(4) 5 \(\sqrt { 2 }\) cm
Answer:
(4) 5 \(\sqrt { 2 }\) cm
Hint:

AB2 = AC2 + BC2
AB2 = 52 + 52
(It is an isosceles triangle)
AB = \(\sqrt { 50 }\) = \(\sqrt{25 \times 2}\)
AB = 5 \(\sqrt { 2 }\)
