Question 9.
Two poles of heights 6 m and 11m stand vertically on a plane ground. If the distance between their feet is 12 m, what is the distance between their tops?
(1) 13 m
(2) 14 m
(3) 15 m
(4) 12.8 m
Answer:
(1) 13 m
Hint:

AC2 (Distance between the two tops)
= AE2 + EC2
= 52 + 122
= 25 + 144= 169
AC = \(\sqrt { 169 }\) = 13 cm
