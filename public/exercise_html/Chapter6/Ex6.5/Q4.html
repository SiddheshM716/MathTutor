Question 4.
If sin θ + cos θ = a and sec θ + cosec θ = b , then the value of b (a2 – 1) is equal to ……………
(1) 2 a
(2) 3 a
(3) 0
(4) 2 ab
Answer:
(1) 2 a
Hint:
b (a2 – 1) = (sec θ + cosec θ) [(sin θ + cos θ)2 – 1]
= \(\frac{1}{\cos \theta}+\frac{1}{\sin \theta}\) [sin2 θ + cos2 θ + 2 sin θ cos θ – 1]
