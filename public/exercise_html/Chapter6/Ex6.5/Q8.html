Question 9.
a cot θ + b cosec θ = p and b cot θ + a cosec θ = q then p2 – q2 is equal to
(1) a2 – b2
(2) b2 – a2
(3) a2 + b2

(2) b2 – a2
(a cot θ + b cosec θ)2 = p2
(b cot θ + a cosec θ )2 = q2

= (a2 – b2)cosec2θ – (a2 – b2) – (a2 – b2) cosec2θ
= b2 – a2
