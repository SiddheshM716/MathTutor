Question 5.
Prove the following identities.
(i) sec4 θ (1 – sin4 θ) – 2 tan2 θ = 1
(ii) \(\frac{\cot \theta-\cos \theta}{\cot \theta+\cos \theta}=\frac{\csc \theta-1}{\csc \theta+1}\)
Answer:
(i) L.H.S = sec4 θ (1 – sin4 θ) – 2 tan2 θ

L.H.S = R.H.S
∴ sec4 θ (1 – sin4 θ) – 2 tan2 θ = 1

(ii) \(\frac{\cot \theta-\cos \theta}{\cot \theta+\cos \theta}=\frac{\csc \theta-1}{\csc \theta+1}\)
