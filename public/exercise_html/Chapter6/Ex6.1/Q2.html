Question 2.
Prove the following identities.
(i) \(\frac{1-\tan ^{2} \theta}{\cot ^{2} \theta-1}\) = tan2 θ
(ii) \(\frac{\cos \theta}{1+\sin \theta}\) = sec θ – tan θ
Answer:
(i) \(\frac{1-\tan ^{2} \theta}{\cot ^{2} \theta-1}\) = tan2 θ

(ii) \(\frac{\cos \theta}{1+\sin \theta}\) = sec θ – tan θ

Aliter:
L.H.S = \(\frac{\cos \theta}{1-\sin \theta}\)
[conjugate (1 – sin θ)]
