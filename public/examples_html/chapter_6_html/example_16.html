Prove that sin² A cos² A = sin² A cos² A

(sin A cos A + cos² A + sin² A)(sin A − cos A)

= sin A cos A (sin A − cos A)

1/(sec A − cosec A) = 1/(1/cos A − 1/sin A) = cos A sin A / (sin A − cos A)

(sin A cos A + 1)(sin A − cos A) / (sin A cos A)

= (sin A cos A + 1)(sec A − cosec A)

sin² A cos² A = sin² A cos² A
