Example 6.14

Prove that

\[ \frac{\sin A \cos A}{\sec A + \tan A - 1} + \frac{\sin A \cos A}{\csc A + \cot A - 1} = \frac{\sin A (\csc A + \cot A - 1) + \cos A (\sec A + \tan A - 1)}{(\sec A + \tan A - 1)(\csc A + \cot A - 1)} \]

\[ = \frac{\sin A \csc A + \sin A \cot A - \sin A + \cos A \sec A + \cos A \tan A - \cos A}{(\sec A + \tan A - 1)(\csc A + \cot A - 1)} \]

\[ = \frac{1 + \cos A - \sin A + 1 + \sin A - \cos A}{\left(\frac{1}{\cos A} + \frac{\sin A}{\cos A} - 1\right)\left(\frac{1}{\sin A} + \frac{\cos A}{\sin A} - 1\right)} \]

\[ = \frac{2}{\left(\frac{1 + \sin A - \cos A}{\cos A}\right)\left(\frac{1 + \cos A - \sin A}{\sin A}\right)} \]

\[ = \frac{2 \sin A \cos A}{(1 + \sin A - \cos A)(1 + \cos A - \sin A)} \]

\[ = \frac{2 \sin A \cos A}{[1 + (\sin A - \cos A)][1 - (\sin A - \cos A)]} \]

\[ = \frac{2 \sin A \cos A}{1 - (\sin A - \cos A)^2} \]

\[ = \frac{2 \sin A \cos A}{1 - (\sin^2 A + \cos^2 A - 2 \sin A \cos A)} \]

\[ = \frac{2 \sin A \cos A}{1 - 1 + 2 \sin A \cos A} \]

\[ = \frac{2 \sin A \cos A}{2 \sin A \cos A} = 1 \]
