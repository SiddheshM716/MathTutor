Example 6.6

Prove that \(\frac{1}{\sin \theta \cos \theta} = \cot \theta\)

Solution:

\[ \frac{\sec \theta - \sin \theta \cos \theta}{\sin \theta \cos \theta} = \frac{\sin \theta}{\sin \theta \cos \theta} \cdot \frac{1}{\cos \theta} = \frac{1 - \sin^2 \theta}{\sin \theta \cos \theta} = \frac{\cos^2 \theta}{\sin \theta \cos \theta} = \cot \theta \]
