Example 6.4

Prove that \(\sec \theta - \cos \theta = \tan \theta \sin \theta\)

Solution:

\[ \sec \theta - \cos \theta = \frac{1}{\cos \theta} - \cos \theta = \frac{1 - \cos^2 \theta}{\cos \theta} = \frac{\sin^2 \theta}{\cos \theta} \]

\[ \frac{\sin^2 \theta}{\cos \theta} = \sin \theta \cdot \frac{\sin \theta}{\cos \theta} = \sin \theta \cdot \tan \theta = \tan \theta \sin \theta \]
