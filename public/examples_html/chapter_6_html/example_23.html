A TV tower stands vertically on a bank of a canal. The tower is watched from a point on the other bank directly opposite to it. The angle of elevation of the top of the tower is 58°. From another point 20 m away from this point on the line joining this point to the foot of the tower, the angle of elevation of the top of the tower is 30°. Find the height of the tower and the width of the canal. (tan 58° = 1.6003)

1

Let AB be the height of the TV tower and BC be the width of the canal.

2

In right triangle ABC, use the tangent formula for angle 58°:

\[ \tan 58° = \frac{AB}{BC} \]

Given \(\tan 58° = 1.6003\), we get:

\[ 1.6003 = \frac{AB}{BC} \quad \text{(1)} \]

3

In right triangle ABD, use the tangent formula for angle 30°:

\[ \tan 30° = \frac{AB}{BD} = \frac{AB}{BC + 20} \]

\[ \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} = \frac{AB}{BC + 20} \quad \text{(2)} \]

4

Divide equation (1) by equation (2):

\[ \frac{1.6003}{\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}} = \frac{BC + 20}{BC} \]

\[ 1.6003 \times \sqrt{3} = \frac{BC + 20}{BC} \]

5

Calculate numerical value (\(\sqrt{3} \approx 1.732\)):

\[ 1.6003 \times 1.732 = 2.7717 \]

\[ 2.7717 = \frac{BC + 20}{BC} \]

6

Solve for BC:

\[ 2.7717 BC = BC + 20 \]

\[ 1.7717 BC = 20 \]

\[ BC = \frac{20}{1.7717} \approx 11.29 \, \text{m} \]

7

Find AB using equation (1):

\[ AB = 1.6003 \times BC \approx 1.6003 \times 11.29 \approx 18.07 \, \text{m} \]

The height of the tower is \(18.07 \, \text{m}\) and the width of the canal is \(11.29 \, \text{m}\).
