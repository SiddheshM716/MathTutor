Example 5.18

Find the equation of a straight line whose

(i) Slope is 5 and y intercept is −9

Solution (i) Given, Slope = 5, y intercept, c = −9

Therefore, equation of a straight line is y = mx + c

y = 5x − 9 ⇒ 5x − y − 9 = 0

(ii) Inclination is 45° and y intercept is 11

Given, θ = 45°, y intercept, c = 11

Slope m = tanθ = tan45° = 1

Therefore, equation of a straight line is of the form y = mx + c

Hence we get, y = x + 11 ⇒ x − y + 11 = 0
