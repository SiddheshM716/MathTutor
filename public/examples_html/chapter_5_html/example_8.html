Example 5.8

(i) What is the slope of a line whose inclination is 30°?

Slope \( m = \tan 30° = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \).

(ii) What is the inclination of a line whose slope is \( \sqrt{3} \)?

Given \( m = \sqrt{3} \), let \( \theta \) be the inclination of the line.

We get, \( \tan \theta = \sqrt{3} \).

Therefore, \( \theta = 60° \).
