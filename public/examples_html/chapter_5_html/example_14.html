Consider the graph representing growth of population (in crores). Find the slope of the line AB and hence estimate the population in the year 2030.

1

Identify the points A and B on the line AB. The points are given as A(2005, 96) and B(2015, 100).

2

Calculate the slope of AB using the formula for slope between two points \((x_1, y_1)\) and \((x_2, y_2)\):

\[

\text{Slope} = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1}

\]

3

Substitute the values of points A and B into the slope formula:

\[

\text{Slope of AB} = \frac{100 - 96}{2015 - 2005} = \frac{4}{10} = \frac{2}{5}

\]

4

Let the population in 2030 be \(k\) crores. Since point C(2030, \(k\)) lies on the line AB, the slope of AC must equal the slope of AB.

5

Set up the equation using the slope formula for points A and C:

\[

\frac{k - 96}{2030 - 2005} = \frac{2}{5}

\]

\[

\frac{k - 96}{25} = \frac{2}{5}

\]

6

Solve for \(k\):

\[

k - 96 = 10

\]

\[

k = 106

\]

The estimated population in 2030 is \(\boxed{106}\) crores.
