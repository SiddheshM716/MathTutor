Find the equation of a straight line passing through (5,7) and is (i) parallel to X axis (ii) parallel to Y axis.

1

For part (i), recall that any line parallel to the X-axis has the form \( y = b \), where \( b \) is a constant.

2

Since the line passes through the point (5,7), substitute \( y = 7 \) into the equation \( y = b \).

3

This gives \( b = 7 \), so the equation of the line is \( y = 7 \).

(i) The equation of the line is \( y = 7 \).

Step 1: For part (ii), recall that any line parallel to the Y-axis has the form \( x = c \), where \( c \) is a constant.

Step 2: Since the line passes through the point (5,7), substitute \( x = 5 \) into the equation \( x = c \).

Step 3: This gives \( c = 5 \), so the equation of the line is \( x = 5 \).

(ii) The equation of the line is \( x = 5 \).
