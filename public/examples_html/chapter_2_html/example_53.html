Example 2.53

A person saved money every year, half as much as he could in the previous year. If he had totally saved ₹7875 in 6 years, then how much did he save in the first year?

Solution: Total amount saved in 6 years is S6 = 7875

Since he saved half as much money every year as he saved in the previous year, we have r = 1/2 < 1

S6 = a(1 - r6) / (1 - r) = 7875 ⇒ a(1 - (1/2)6) / (1 - 1/2) = 7875

a(1 - 1/64) / (1/2) = 7875 ⇒ a(63/64) / (1/2) = 7875

a(63/32) = 7875 ⇒ a = 7875 × 32 / 63

a = 4000

The amount saved in the first year is ₹4000.
