Find the sum of the first 15 terms of the A.P. 8, 6.5.

1

Identify the given values.

First term \( a = 8 \), common difference \( d = 6.5 - 8 = -1.5 \), and number of terms \( n = 15 \).

2

Recall the formula for the sum of the first \( n \) terms of an A.P.

The formula is:

\[ S_n = \frac{n}{2} [2a + (n - 1)d] \]

3

Substitute the values into the formula and calculate.

\[ S_{15} = \frac{15}{2} [2 \times 8 + (15 - 1)(-1.5)] \]

\[ S_{15} = \frac{15}{2} [16 + 14 \times (-1.5)] \]

\[ S_{15} = \frac{15}{2} [16 - 21] \]

\[ S_{15} = \frac{15}{2} \times (-5) \]

\[ S_{15} = -37.5 \]

The sum of the first 15 terms is \(-37.5\).
