1

Identify the first term (a) and common difference (d) of the A.P.

Given the sequence 3, 15, 27, 39,..., we have:

First term \( a = 3 \)

Common difference \( d = 15 - 3 = 12 \)

2

Recall the formula for the n

th

term of an A.P.

The n

th

term \( t_n \) is given by:

\[ t_n = a + (n - 1)d \]

3

Calculate the 15

th

term \( t_{15} \).

\[ t_{15} = 3 + (15 - 1) \times 12 \]

\[ t_{15} = 3 + 14 \times 12 \]

\[ t_{15} = 3 + 168 \]

\[ t_{15} = 171 \]

4

Calculate the 24

th

term \( t_{24} \).

\[ t_{24} = 3 + (24 - 1) \times 12 \]

\[ t_{24} = 3 + 23 \times 12 \]

\[ t_{24} = 3 + 276 \]

\[ t_{24} = 279 \]

5

Derive the general term \( t_n \).

\[ t_n = 3 + (n - 1) \times 12 \]

\[ t_n = 3 + 12n - 12 \]

\[ t_n = 12n - 9 \]

General term \( t_n = 12n - 9 \).
