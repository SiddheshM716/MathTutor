Example 2.44

The product of three consecutive terms of a Geometric Progression is 343 and their sum is 91⁄3. Find the three terms.

Solution: Since the product of 3 consecutive terms is given, we can take them as a⁄r, a, ar.

Product of the terms = 343

a × a × ar = 343

a3 = 343

a = 7

Sum of the terms = 91⁄3

a⁄r + a + ar = 91⁄3

7⁄r + 7 + 7r = 91⁄3

3 + 3r + 3r2 = 13r

3r2 - 10r + 3 = 0

(3r - 1)(r - 3) = 0

r = 3 or r = 1⁄3

If a = 7, r = 3 then the three terms are 7⁄3, 7, 21.

If a = 7, r = 1⁄3 then the three terms are 21, 7, 7⁄3.
