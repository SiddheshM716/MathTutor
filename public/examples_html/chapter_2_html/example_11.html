Find the remainders when 70004 and 778 are divided by 7.

1

Analyze 70004 modulo 7.

Since 70000 is divisible by 7, we have \(70000 \equiv 0 \pmod{7}\).

2

Add the remaining term.

\(70000 + 4 \equiv 0 + 4 \pmod{7}\).

3

Simplify the congruence.

\(70004 \equiv 4 \pmod{7}\).

The remainder when 70004 is divided by 7 is \(\boxed{4}\).
