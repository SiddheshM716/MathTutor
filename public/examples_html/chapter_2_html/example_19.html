Example 2.19

Find the next three terms of the sequences:

(i) 2, 6, 10, 14,...

In the above sequence, the numerators are the same and the denominator is increased by 4.

So the next three terms are:

a1 = 1 / (14 + 4) = 1 / 18

a2 = 1 / (18 + 4) = 1 / 22

a3 = 1 / (22 + 4) = 1 / 26

(ii) 5, 2, -1, -4,...

Here each term is decreased by 3. So the next three terms are -7, -10, -13.

(iii) 1, 0.1, 0.01,...

Here each term is divided by 10. Hence, the next three terms are:

a1 = 0.01 / 10 = 0.001

a2 = 0.001 / 10 = 0.0001

a3 = 0.0001 / 10 = 0.00001
