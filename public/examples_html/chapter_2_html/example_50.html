Find the rational form of the number 0.6666...

1

Express the repeating decimal as an infinite series:

0.6666... = 0.6 + 0.06 + 0.006 + 0.0006 + ...

2

Identify the series as a geometric progression (G.P.) with first term \( a = 0.6 \) and common ratio \( r = 0.1 \). Since \( -1 < r < 1 \), the infinite G.P. formula applies.

3

Use the infinite G.P. sum formula \( S = \frac{a}{1 - r} \):

\[ 0.6666... = \frac{0.6}{1 - 0.1} = \frac{0.6}{0.9} \]

4

Simplify the fraction:

\[ \frac{0.6}{0.9} = \frac{6}{9} = \frac{2}{3} \]

The rational form of 0.6666... is \( \frac{2}{3} \).
