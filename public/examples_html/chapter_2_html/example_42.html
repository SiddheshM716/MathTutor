Find the 8

th

term of the G.P. 9, 3, 1,...

1

Identify the given values from the geometric progression (G.P.).

The first term \( a = 9 \).

The common ratio \( r = \frac{3}{9} = \frac{1}{3} \).

2

Recall the formula for the \( n^{th} \) term of a G.P.:

\[ t_n = ar^{n-1} \]

3

Substitute the values into the formula to find the 8

th

term (\( t_8 \)):

\[ t_8 = 9 \times \left( \frac{1}{3} \right)^{7} \]

\[ t_8 = 9 \times \frac{1}{2187} \]

\[ t_8 = \frac{9}{2187} = \frac{1}{243} \]

The 8

th

term of the G.P. is \( \frac{1}{243} \).
