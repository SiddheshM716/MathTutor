Example 2.47

Find the first term of a G.P. in which \( S_6 = 4095 \) and \( r = 4 \).

Solution:

Common ratio \( r = 4 > 1 \), Sum of first 6 terms \( S_6 = 4095 \).

Hence, \( S_6 = a \frac{r^6 - 1}{r - 1} = 4095 \).

\( a \frac{4^6 - 1}{4 - 1} = 4095 \)

\( a \frac{4096 - 1}{3} = 4095 \)

\( a \frac{4095}{3} = 4095 \)

\( a \times 1365 = 4095 \)

First term \( a = 3 \).
