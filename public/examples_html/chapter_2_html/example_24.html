Write an A.P. whose first term is 20 and common difference is 8.

1

Identify the given values.

First term \( a = 20 \), common difference \( d = 8 \).

2

Recall the formula for an Arithmetic Progression (A.P.).

An A.P. is given by the sequence: \( a, a + d, a + 2d, a + 3d, \ldots \).

3

Substitute the given values into the formula.

The sequence becomes: \( 20, 20 + 8, 20 + 2(8), 20 + 3(8), \ldots \).

The required A.P. is \( 20, 28, 36, 44, \ldots \).
