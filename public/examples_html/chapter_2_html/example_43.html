Example 2.43

In a Geometric progression, the 4th term is 8243 and the 7th term is 64729. Find the Geometric Progression.

Solution:

4th term, t4=8243⇒ar3=8243 ...(1)

7th term, t7=64729⇒ar6=64729 ...(2)

Dividing (2) by (1) we get, ar6ar3=647298243⇒r3=827⇒r=23

Substituting the value of r in (1), we get a×233=8243⇒a=278

Therefore the Geometric Progression is a,ar,ar2,... That is, 278,98,38,...
