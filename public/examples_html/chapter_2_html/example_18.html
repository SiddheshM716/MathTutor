\( 71 \equiv 2z \ (\text{mod} \ 8) \)

\( 78 + 2 \equiv 3 \ (\text{mod} \ 5) \)

\( 89 \equiv (2 + 3) \ (\text{mod} \ 4) \)

Solve \( 71 \equiv 2z \ (\text{mod} \ 8) \).

First, find \( 71 \mod 8 \):

\( 71 \div 8 = 8 \) with remainder \( 7 \), so \( 71 \equiv 7 \ (\text{mod} \ 8) \).

2

The equation becomes \( 7 \equiv 2z \ (\text{mod} \ 8) \).

We need to find the smallest positive \( z \) such that \( 2z \equiv 7 \ (\text{mod} \ 8) \).
