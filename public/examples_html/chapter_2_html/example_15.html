Compute \(2z\), such that \(10^2 \equiv z \pmod{19}\).

1

Compute \(10^2 \pmod{19}\).

\[10^2 = 100\]

2

Find the remainder when 100 is divided by 19.

\[100 \div 19 = 5 \text{ with a remainder of } 5\]

3

Thus, \(10^2 \equiv 5 \pmod{19}\), so \(z = 5\).

4

Compute \(2z\).

\[2z = 2 \times 5 = 10\]

\(2z = 10\)
