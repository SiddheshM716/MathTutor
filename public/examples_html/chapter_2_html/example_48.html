How many terms of the series \(1 + 4 + 16 + \dots\) make the sum 1365?

1

Identify the given values. The first term \(a = 1\), common ratio \(r = 4\), and sum \(S = 1365\).

2

Use the formula for the sum of a geometric series when \(r > 1\):

\[

S = \frac{a(r^n - 1)}{r - 1}

\]

3

Substitute the known values into the formula and solve for \(n\):

\[

\frac{1(4^n - 1)}{4 - 1} = 1365

\]

\[

4^n - 1 = 4095

\]

\[

4^n = 4096

\]

\[

4^n = 4^6

\]

\[

n = 6

\]

The number of terms required is \(\boxed{6}\).
