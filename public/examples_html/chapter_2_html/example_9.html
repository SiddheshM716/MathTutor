Is $7 \times 5 \times 3 \times 2 + 3$ a composite number? Justify your answer.

1

Expand the given expression to identify common factors.

$7 \times 5 \times 3 \times 2 + 3$

2

Factor out the common term (3) from the expression.

$3 \times (7 \times 5 \times 2 + 1) = 3 \times 71$
