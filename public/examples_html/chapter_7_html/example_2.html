The curved surface area of a right circular cylinder of height 14 cm is 88 cm². Find the diameter of the cylinder.

1

Given the curved surface area (C.S.A.) of the cylinder is 88 cm² and height (h) is 14 cm.

2

The formula for the curved surface area of a right circular cylinder is:

\[ \text{C.S.A.} = 2\pi rh \]

3

Substitute the known values into the formula:

\[ 2 \times \pi \times r \times 14 = 88 \]

4

Simplify the equation to solve for radius (r):

\[ r = \frac{88 \times 7}{22 \times 14} \]

5

Calculate the radius:

\[ r = \frac{616}{308} = 2 \, \text{cm} \]

The diameter of the cylinder is \( 2 \times 2 = 4 \, \text{cm} \).
