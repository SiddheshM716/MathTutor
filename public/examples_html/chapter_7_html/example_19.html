The volume of a solid right circular cone is 11088 cm³. If its height is 24 cm, find the radius of the cone.

1

Let \( r \) be the radius and \( h = 24 \) cm be the height of the cone. The volume \( V \) of the cone is given as 11088 cm³.

2

The formula for the volume of a cone is:

\[ V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h \]

3

Substitute the known values into the formula:

\[ \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{22}{7} \times r^2 \times 24 = 11088 \]

4

Simplify the equation step by step:

\[ \frac{22}{7} \times r^2 \times 8 = 11088 \]

\[ r^2 = \frac{11088 \times 7}{22 \times 8} \]

\[ r^2 = 441 \]

The radius of the cone is \( r = 21 \) cm.
