Example 7.13

The slant height of a frustum of a cone is 5 cm and the radii of its ends are 4 cm and 1 cm. Find its curved surface area.

Solution: Let \( l \), \( R \), and \( r \) be the slant height, top radius, and bottom radius of the frustum. Given that, \( l = 5 \) cm, \( R = 4 \) cm, \( r = 1 \) cm.

Now, C.S.A. of the frustum \( = \pi (R + r) l \) sq. units

\( = \frac{22}{7} \times (4 + 1) \times 5 \)

\( = \frac{22}{7} \times 5 \times 5 \)

\( = \frac{550}{7} \)

Therefore, C.S.A. \( = 78.57 \) cmÂ²
