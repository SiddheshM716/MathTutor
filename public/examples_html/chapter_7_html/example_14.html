An industrial metallic bucket is in the shape of the frustum of a right circular cone whose top and bottom diameters are 10 m and 4 m and whose height is 4 m. Find the curved and total surface area of the bucket.

1

Identify the given parameters.

Given:

Top diameter = 10 m → Top radius \( R = 5 \) m

Bottom diameter = 4 m → Bottom radius \( r = 2 \) m

Height \( h = 4 \) m

2

Calculate the slant height \( l \) using the formula:

\[ l = \sqrt{h^2 + (R - r)^2} \]

3

Substitute the values into the formula:

\[ l = \sqrt{4^2 + (5 - 2)^2} = \sqrt{16 + 9} = \sqrt{25} = 5 \text{ m} \]

4

Calculate the Curved Surface Area (C.S.A.) using the formula:

\[ C.S.A. = \pi(R + r)l \]

5

Substitute the values:

\[ C.S.A. = \pi(5 + 2) \times 5 = 35\pi \approx 110 \text{ m}^2 \]

6

Calculate the Total Surface Area (T.S.A.) using the formula:

\[ T.S.A. = \pi(R + r)l + \pi R^2 + \pi r^2 \]

7

Substitute the values:

\[ T.S.A. = \pi[(5 + 2) \times 5 + 5^2 + 2^2] = \pi[35 + 25 + 4] = 64\pi \approx 201.14 \text{ m}^2 \]

Curved Surface Area (C.S.A.) = 110 m²

Total Surface Area (T.S.A.) = 201.14 m²
