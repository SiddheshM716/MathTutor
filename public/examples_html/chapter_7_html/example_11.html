Example 7.11

The internal and external radii of a hollow hemispherical shell are 3 m and 5 m respectively. Find the T.S.A. and C.S.A. of the shell.

Let the internal and external radii of the hemispherical shell be r and R respectively.

Given that, R = 5 m, r = 3 m

C.S.A. of the shell = 2π(R² + r²) sq. units

= 2 × π × (25 + 9) = 213.71

T.S.A. of the shell = π(3R² + r²) sq. units

= π × (75 + 9) = 264

Therefore, C.S.A. = 213.71 m² and T.S.A. = 264 m².
