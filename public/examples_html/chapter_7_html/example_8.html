Find the diameter of a sphere whose surface area is 154 m

2

.

1

Let \( r \) be the radius of the sphere. The surface area of the sphere is given as 154 m

2

.

2

The formula for the surface area of a sphere is \( 4\pi r^2 \).

\[ 4\pi r^2 = 154 \]

3

Substitute \( \pi = \frac{22}{7} \) and solve for \( r^2 \):

\[ 4 \times \frac{22}{7} \times r^2 = 154 \]

\[ r^2 = \frac{154 \times 7}{4 \times 22} \]

\[ r^2 = \frac{1078}{88} \]

\[ r^2 = \frac{49}{4} \]

4

Take the square root of both sides to find \( r \):

\[ r = \frac{7}{2} \text{ m} \]

5

The diameter \( d \) is twice the radius:

\[ d = 2 \times \frac{7}{2} = 7 \text{ m} \]

The diameter of the sphere is \( 7 \text{ m} \).
