Example 7.8

Find the diameter of a sphere whose surface area is 154 m2.

Solution: Let \( r \) be the radius of the sphere.

Given that, surface area of sphere = 154 m2.

\[ 4\pi r^2 = 154 \]

\[ 4 \times \frac{22}{7} \times r^2 = 154 \]

\[ r^2 = \frac{154 \times 7}{4 \times 22} \]

\[ r^2 = \frac{1078}{88} \]

\[ r^2 = \frac{49}{4} \]

\[ r = \frac{7}{2} \]

Therefore, diameter is 7 m.
