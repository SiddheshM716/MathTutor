If the total surface area of a cone of radius 7 cm is 704 cm², then find its slant height.

1

Given the radius \( r = 7 \) cm and total surface area \( T.S.A. = 704 \) cm².

2

The formula for the total surface area of a cone is:

\[ T.S.A. = \pi r (l + r) \]

3

Substitute the known values into the formula:

\[ 704 = \pi \times 7 (l + 7) \]

4

Simplify the equation (using \( \pi = \frac{22}{7} \)):

\[ 704 = \frac{22}{7} \times 7 (l + 7) \]

\[ 704 = 22 (l + 7) \]

5

Solve for \( l \):

\[ \frac{704}{22} = l + 7 \]

\[ 32 = l + 7 \]

\[ l = 25 \]

The slant height of the cone is \( 25 \) cm.
