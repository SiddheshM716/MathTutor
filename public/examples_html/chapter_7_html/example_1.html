A cylindrical drum has a height of 20 cm and base radius of 14 cm. Find its curved surface area and the total surface area.

1

Given the height \( h = 20 \) cm and radius \( r = 14 \) cm of the cylinder.

2

The formula for the curved surface area (C.S.A.) of a cylinder is \( 2\pi rh \).

3

Calculate the C.S.A.:

\[

2 \times \pi \times 14 \times 20 = 2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 14 \times 20 = 1760 \text{ cm}^2

\]

4

The formula for the total surface area (T.S.A.) of a cylinder is \( 2\pi r(h + r) \).

5

Calculate the T.S.A.:

\[

2 \times \pi \times 14 \times (20 + 14) = 2 \times \frac{22}{7} \times 14 \times 34 = 2992 \text{ cm}^2

\]

Curved Surface Area = \( 1760 \text{ cm}^2 \), Total Surface Area = \( 2992 \text{ cm}^2 \).
