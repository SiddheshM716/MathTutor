Example 7.1

A cylindrical drum has a height of 20 cm and base radius of 14 cm. Find its curved surface area and the total surface area.

Solution Given that, height of the cylinder h = 20cm; radius r=14 cm

Now, C.S.A. of the cylinder = 2πrh sq. units

C.S.A. of the cylinder = 2 × π × 14 × 20 = 2 × 22/7 × 14 × 20 = 1760 cm²

T.S.A. of the cylinder = 2πr(h + r) sq. units

= 2 × π × 14 × (20 + 14) = 2 × 22/7 × 14 × 34

= 2992 cm²

Therefore, C.S.A. = 1760 cm² and T.S.A. = 2992 cm²
