Example 4.28

If radii of two concentric circles are 4 cm and 5 cm then find the length of the chord of one circle which is a tangent to the other circle.

Solution: OA = 4 cm, OB = 5 cm; also OA ⊥ BC.

OB² = OA² + AB²

5² = 4² + AB² gives AB² = 9

Therefore AB = 3 cm

BC = 2AB hence BC = 2 × 3 = 6 cm
