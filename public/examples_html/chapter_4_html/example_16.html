Let BD = x cm, then DC = (6 - x) cm since BC = 6 cm.

2

Apply the Angle Bisector Theorem, which states \(\frac{AB}{AC} = \frac{BD}{DC}\).

3

Substitute the given values into the theorem:

\[

\frac{10}{14} = \frac{x}{6 - x}

\]

Simplify the fraction:

\[

\frac{5}{7} = \frac{x}{6 - x}

\]

Cross-multiply to solve for x:

\[

5(6 - x) = 7x

\]

\[

30 - 5x = 7x

\]

\[

12x = 30

\]

\[

x = 2.5 \text{ cm}

\]

BD = 2.5 cm, DC = 6 - 2.5 = 3.5 cm.
