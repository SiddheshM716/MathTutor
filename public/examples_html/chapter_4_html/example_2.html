Is ΔABC ~ ΔPQR?

In ΔABC and ΔPQR,

\(\frac{PQ}{AB} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2}\), \(\frac{QR}{BC} = \frac{4}{10} = \frac{2}{5}\)

1

Compare the ratios of corresponding sides of ΔABC and ΔPQR.

2

Calculate the ratio \(\frac{PQ}{AB}\):

\[

\frac{PQ}{AB} = \frac{3}{6} = \frac{1}{2}

\]

3

Calculate the ratio \(\frac{QR}{BC}\):

\[

\frac{QR}{BC} = \frac{4}{10} = \frac{2}{5}

\]

4

Check if the ratios are equal:

\[

\frac{1}{2} \neq \frac{2}{5}

\]

Since the corresponding sides are not proportional, ΔABC is not similar to ΔPQR.
