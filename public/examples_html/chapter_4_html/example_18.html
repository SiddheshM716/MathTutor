Example 4.18

Construct a triangle ΔPQR such that QR = 5 cm, ∠P = 30° and the altitude from P to QR is of length 4.2 cm.

Solution

Construction

  1. Draw a line segment QR = 5 cm.
  2. At Q draw QE such that ∠RQE = 30°.
  3. At Q draw QF such that ∠EQF = 90°.
  4. Draw the perpendicular bisector XY to QR which intersects QF at O and QR at G.
  5. With O as centre and OQ as radius draw a circle.
  6. From G mark an arc in the line XY at M, such that GM = 4.2 cm.
  7. Draw AB through M which is parallel to QR.
  8. AB meets the circle at P and S.
  9. Join QP and RP. Then ΔPQR is the required triangle.
