Construct a triangle ΔPQR such that QR = 5 cm, ∠P = 30° and the altitude from P to QR is of length 4.2 cm.

1

Draw a line segment QR = 5 cm.

2

At point Q, construct angle ∠RQE = 30°.

3

At point Q, construct angle ∠EQF = 90°.

4

Draw the perpendicular bisector XY of QR, intersecting QF at O and QR at G.

5

With O as the center and OQ as the radius, draw a circle.

6

From point G, mark an arc on line XY at M such that GM = 4.2 cm.

7

Draw line AB through M, parallel to QR.

8

Line AB intersects the circle at points P and S.

9

Join QP and RP to form the required triangle ΔPQR.
