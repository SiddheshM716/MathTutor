Draw a circle of radius 3 cm. Take a point P on this circle and draw a tangent at P.

1

Given the radius of the circle is 3 cm.

2

To construct the tangent, first draw a circle with center O and radius 3 cm.

3

Mark a point P on the circumference of the circle and draw the line segment OP.

4

Construct a line TT' perpendicular to OP at point P. This line TT' is the required tangent at point P.

The tangent TT' is successfully constructed at point P on the circle of radius 3 cm.
