Example 4.29

Draw a circle of radius 3 cm. Take a point P on this circle and draw a tangent at P.

Solution: Given, radius r = 3 cm

Construction:

  1. Draw a circle with centre at O of radius 3 cm.
  2. Take a point P on the circle. Join OP.
  3. Draw a perpendicular line TT' to OP which passes through P.
  4. TT' is the required tangent.
