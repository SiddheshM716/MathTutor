Example 4.6

In Fig.4.21, QA and PB are perpendiculars to AB. If AO = 10 cm, BO = 6 cm and PB = 9 cm. Find AQ.

Solution: In ΔAOQ and ΔBOP, ∠OAQ = ∠OBP = 90°

∠AOQ = ∠BOP (Vertically opposite angles)

Therefore, by AA Criterion of similarity, ΔAOQ ~ ΔBOP

\(\frac{AQ}{BO} = \frac{AO}{BP}\)

\(\frac{AQ}{6} = \frac{10}{9}\)

\(AQ = \frac{10 \times 6}{9} = \frac{60}{9} = 6.\overline{6} \text{ cm}\)
