Example 4.14

In the figure, DE || AC and DC || AP. Prove that \(\frac{BE}{EC} = \frac{BC}{CP}\).

Solution: In \(\triangle BPA\), we have DC || AP. By Basic Proportionality Theorem, we have \(\frac{BC}{CP} = \frac{BD}{DA}\). (1)

In \(\triangle BCA\), we have DE || AC. By Basic Proportionality Theorem, we have \(\frac{BE}{EC} = \frac{BD}{DA}\). (2)

From (1) and (2) we get, \(\frac{BE}{EC} = \frac{BC}{CP}\). Hence proved.
