Example 4.15

In the Fig.4.39, AD is the bisector of ∠A. If BD = 4cm, DC = 3cm and AB = 6cm, find AC.

Solution: In ΔABC, AD is the bisector of ∠A.

By Angle Bisector Theorem:

\(\frac{BD}{DC} = \frac{AB}{AC}\)

\(\frac{4}{3} = \frac{6}{AC}\)

\(4 \times AC = 6 \times 3\)

\(4AC = 18\)

\(AC = \frac{18}{4} = 4.5\) cm
