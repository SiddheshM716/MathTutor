If \( f(x) = 2x + 3 \), \( g(x) = 1 - 2x \) and \( h(x) = 3x \). Prove that \( f \circ (g \circ h) = (f \circ g) \circ h \).

1

Given the functions \( f(x) = 2x + 3 \), \( g(x) = 1 - 2x \), and \( h(x) = 3x \).

2

First, compute \( (f \circ g)(x) \). The composition \( (f \circ g)(x) \) is defined as \( f(g(x)) \).

\[ (f \circ g)(x) = f(g(x)) = f(1 - 2x) = 2(1 - 2x) + 3 = 5 - 4x \]

3

Next, compute \( (f \circ g) \circ h(x) \). This is \( (f \circ g)(h(x)) \).

\[ (f \circ g) \circ h(x) = (f \circ g)(3x) = 5 - 4(3x) = 5 - 12x \quad \text{(1)} \]

4

Now, compute \( (g \circ h)(x) \). The composition \( (g \circ h)(x) \) is defined as \( g(h(x)) \).

\[ (g \circ h)(x) = g(3x) = 1 - 2(3x) = 1 - 6x \]

5

Finally, compute \( f \circ (g \circ h)(x) \). This is \( f((g \circ h)(x)) \).

\[ f \circ (g \circ h)(x) = f(1 - 6x) = 2(1 - 6x) + 3 = 5 - 12x \quad \text{(2)} \]

From (1) and (2), we conclude that \( (f \circ g) \circ h = f \circ (g \circ h) \).
