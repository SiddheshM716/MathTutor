Example 1.2

If \( A \times B = \{(3,2), (3,4), (5,2), (5,4)\} \) then find \( A \) and \( B \).

\( A \times B = \{(3,2), (3,4), (5,2), (5,4)\} \)

We have \( A = \{ \text{set of all first coordinates of elements of } A \times B \} \). Thus, \( A = \{3,5\} \).

\( B = \{ \text{set of all second coordinates of elements of } A \times B \} \). Thus, \( B = \{2,4\} \).

Thus \( A = \{3,5\} \) and \( B = \{2,4\} \).
