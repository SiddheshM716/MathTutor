If \( A \times B = \{(3,2), (3,4), (5,2), (5,4)\} \) then find \( A \) and \( B \).

1

Identify the first coordinates of all ordered pairs in \( A \times B \) to determine set \( A \).

The first coordinates are \( 3, 3, 5, 5 \).

2

Remove duplicates to form set \( A \).

Thus, \( A = \{3, 5\} \).

3

Identify the second coordinates of all ordered pairs in \( A \times B \) to determine set \( B \).

The second coordinates are \( 2, 4, 2, 4 \).

4

Remove duplicates to form set \( B \).

Thus, \( B = \{2, 4\} \).

\( A = \{3, 5\} \) and \( B = \{2, 4\} \).
