Find \( k \) if \( f \circ f(k) = 5 \) where \( f(k) = 2k - 1 \).

1

Understand the composition \( f \circ f(k) \), which means \( f(f(k)) \).

2

Substitute \( f(k) = 2k - 1 \) into \( f(f(k)) \).

3

Apply the function \( f \) again to \( f(k) \):

\[ f(f(k)) = 2(2k - 1) - 1 = 4k - 2 - 1 = 4k - 3 \]

4

Set the composition equal to 5 and solve for \( k \):

\[ 4k - 3 = 5 \]

\[ 4k = 8 \]

\[ k = 2 \]

\( k = 2 \)
