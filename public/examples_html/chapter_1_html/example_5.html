The arrow diagram shows a relationship between the sets P and Q. Write the relation in (i) Set builder form (ii) Roster form (iii) What is the domain and range of R.

1

For part (i), we observe the relationship between elements of P and Q from the arrow diagram. The pattern shows each element y in Q is 2 less than its corresponding element x in P.

2

Using the set builder form notation, we express this relationship as $\{(x,y) | y = x - 2, x \in P, y \in Q\}$.

3

For part (ii), we list the specific ordered pairs from the diagram where this relationship holds: $(5,3)$, $(6,4)$, and $(7,5)$.

4

For part (iii), the domain is the set of all first elements from the ordered pairs, and the range is the set of all second elements.

(i) $R = \{(x,y) | y = x - 2, x \in P, y \in Q\}$

(ii) $R = \{(5,3), (6,4), (7,5)\}$

(iii) Domain of R = $\{5, 6, 7\}$, Range of R = $\{3, 4, 5\}$
