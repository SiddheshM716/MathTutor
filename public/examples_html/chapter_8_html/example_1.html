Find the range and coefficient of range of the following data: 25, 67, 48, 53, 18, 39, 44.

1

Identify the largest and smallest values in the data set.

Largest value \( L = 67 \); Smallest value \( S = 18 \).

2

Calculate the range using the formula:

Range \( R = L - S \).

3

Substitute the values and compute the range.

\( R = 67 - 18 = 49 \).

4

Calculate the coefficient of range using the formula:

Coefficient of range \( = \frac{L - S}{L + S} \).

5

Substitute the values and compute the coefficient of range.

\( \frac{67 - 18}{67 + 18} = \frac{49}{85} \approx 0.576 \).

The range is \( 49 \), and the coefficient of range is approximately \( 0.576 \).
