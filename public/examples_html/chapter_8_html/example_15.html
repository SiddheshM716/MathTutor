The mean of a data is 25.6 and its coefficient of variation is 18.75. Find the standard deviation.

1

Given the mean (\(\bar{x}\)) = 25.6 and coefficient of variation (C.V.) = 18.75.

2

The formula for coefficient of variation is:

\[ C.V. = \frac{\sigma}{\bar{x}} \times 100\% \]

3

Substitute the given values into the formula:

\[ 18.75 = \frac{\sigma}{25.6} \times 100 \]

Solve for \(\sigma\):

\[ \sigma = \frac{18.75 \times 25.6}{100} = 4.8 \]

The standard deviation (\(\sigma\)) is 4.8.
