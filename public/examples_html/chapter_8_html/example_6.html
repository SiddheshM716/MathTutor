The marks scored by 10 students: 25, 29, 30, 33, 35, 37, 38, 40, 44, 48. Find the standard deviation.

1

Since the mean (35.9) is not an integer, we use the assumed mean method with A = 35 and n = 10.

2

Calculate the deviations (dᵢ = xᵢ - 35) and their squares (dᵢ²) as shown in the table.

3

Sum the deviations (Σdᵢ = 9) and their squares (Σdᵢ² = 453).

4

Use the standard deviation formula for assumed mean method:

\[

\sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\Sigma d_i^2}{n} - \left(\frac{\Sigma d_i}{n}\right)^2} = \sqrt{\frac{453}{10} - \left(\frac{9}{10}\right)^2}

\]
