Example 8.28

If A and B are two events such that P(A) = 14, P(B) = 12 and P(A and B) = 18, find (i) P(A or B) (ii) P(not A and not B).

Solution:

(i) P(A or B) = P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) − P(A ∩ B)

P(A or B) = 14 + 12 − 18 = 58

(ii) P(not A and not B) = P(A̅ ∩ B̅) = 1 − P(A ∪ B)

P(not A and not B) = 1 − 58 = 38
