Find the range of the following distribution.

Age (in years)

16-18

18-20

20-22

22-24

24-26

26-28

Number of students

0

4

6

8

2

2

1

Identify the largest value \( L \) and smallest value \( S \) from the given distribution.

Here, \( L = 28 \) (highest age limit) and \( S = 18 \) (lowest age limit).

2

Use the range formula:

\[ R = L - S \]

3

Substitute the values and calculate:

\[ R = 28 - 18 = 10 \]

The range is \( 10 \) years.
