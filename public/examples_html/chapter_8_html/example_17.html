Express the sample space for rolling two dice using a tree diagram.

1

Understand that each die has 6 faces, numbered from 1 to 6. When rolling two dice, the outcome of the first die does not affect the outcome of the second die.

2

Construct a tree diagram where the first level represents the outcomes of the first die (1 through 6), and the second level represents the outcomes of the second die (1 through 6) for each branch of the first die.

3

List all possible ordered pairs (a, b), where 'a' is the outcome of the first die and 'b' is the outcome of the second die.
