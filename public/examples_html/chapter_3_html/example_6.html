Solve the system of equations:

\[ 3x + y - 3z = 1 \quad \text{(1)} \]

\[ -2x - y + 2z = 1 \quad \text{(2)} \]

\[ -x - y + 2z = 2 \quad \text{(3)} \]

1

Add equations (1) and (2) to eliminate \( y \):

\[ 3x + y - 3z = 1 \]

\[ -2x - y + 2z = 1 \]

\[ ------------------- \]

\[ x - z = 2 \quad \text{(4)} \]

2

Add equations (1) and (3) to eliminate \( y \) again:

\[ 3x + y - 3z = 1 \]

\[ -x - y + 2z = 2 \]

\[ ------------------- \]

\[ 2x - z = 3 \quad \text{(5)} \]

3

Subtract \( 2 \times \text{(4)} \) from equation (5) to eliminate \( x \):

\[ 2x - z = 3 \]

\[ 2x - 2z = 4 \quad \text{(from } 2 \times \text{(4))} \]

\[ ------------------- \]

\[ 0 = -1 \]

The system is inconsistent and has no solution.
