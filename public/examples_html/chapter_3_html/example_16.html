Find:

(i) \(\frac{14x^7}{3y} \div \frac{7x}{16y}\)

(ii) \(\frac{x^2 - 16}{x + 4} \div \frac{x - 4}{x + 4}\)

(iii) \(\frac{16x^2 - 20x - 3}{3x^2 - 2x - 1} \div \frac{8x^2 + 11x + 3}{3x^2 - 11x - 4}\)

1

Rewrite the division as multiplication by the reciprocal.

For (i):

\(\frac{14x^7}{3y} \div \frac{7x}{16y} = \frac{14x^7}{3y} \times \frac{16y}{7x}\)

2

Simplify the expression by canceling common terms.

\(\frac{14x^7 \cdot 16y}{3y \cdot 7x} = \frac{224x^7 y}{21xy}\)

3

Cancel \(y\) and simplify \(x^7 / x = x^6\), and \(224/21 = 32/3\).

Final simplified form: \(32x^6\)

(i) \(32x^6\)

---

Step 1: Rewrite the division as multiplication by the reciprocal.

For (ii):

\(\frac{x^2 - 16}{x + 4} \div \frac{x - 4}{x + 4} = \frac{x^2 - 16}{x + 4} \times \frac{x + 4}{x - 4}\)

Step 2: Factor \(x^2 - 16\) as \((x + 4)(x - 4)\).

\(\frac{(x + 4)(x - 4)}{x + 4} \times \frac{x + 4}{x - 4}\)

Step 3: Cancel \((x + 4)\) and \((x - 4)\).

Simplified form: \(x + 4\)

(ii) \(x + 4\)

---

Step 1: Rewrite the division as multiplication by the reciprocal.

For (iii):

\(\frac{16x^2 - 20x - 3}{3x^2 - 2x - 1} \div \frac{8x^2 + 11x + 3}{3x^2 - 11x - 4} = \frac{16x^2 - 20x - 3}{3x^2 - 2x - 1} \times \frac{3x^2 - 11x - 4}{8x^2 + 11x + 3}\)

Step 2: Factor all quadratic expressions.

Numerator 1: \(16x^2 - 20x - 3 = (8x + 3)(2x - 1)\)

Denominator 1: \(3x^2 - 2x - 1 = (3x + 1)(x - 1)\)

Numerator 2: \(3x^2 - 11x - 4 = (3x + 1)(x - 4)\)

Denominator 2: \(8x^2 + 11x + 3 = (8x + 3)(x + 1)\)

Step 3: Substitute factored forms and cancel common terms.

\(\frac{(8x + 3)(2x - 1)}{(3x + 1)(x - 1)} \times \frac{(3x + 1)(x - 4)}{(8x + 3)(x + 1)} = \frac{(2x - 1)(x - 4)}{(x - 1)(x + 1)}\)

Step 4: Expand the remaining terms.

\(\frac{2x^2 - 9x + 4}{x^2 - 1}\)

(iii) \(\frac{2x^2 - 9x + 4}{x^2 - 1}\)
