Example 3.12

Find the LCM of the following

(i) 8x2y3, 48x3y2

(ii) 5x2 − 10, 5x2 − 20

(iii) x4 − 1, x2 − 2x + 1

(iv) x3 − 27, (x − 3)2, x2 − 9

Solution

(i) 8x2y3, 48x3y2

LCM (8, 48) = 48

LCM (x2y3, x3y2) = x3y3

Therefore, LCM (8x2y3, 48x3y2) = 48x3y3

(ii) (5x2 − 10), (5x2 − 20)

5x2 − 10 = 5(x2 − 2)

5x2 − 20 = 5(x2 − 4) = 5(x + 2)(x − 2)

Therefore, LCM [(5x2 − 10), (5x2 − 20)] = 5(x + 2)(x − 2)

(iii) (x4 − 1), (x2 − 2x + 1)

x4 − 1 = (x2 + 1)(x2 − 1) = (x2 + 1)(x + 1)(x − 1)

x2 − 2x + 1 = (x − 1)2

Therefore, LCM [(x4 − 1), (x2 − 2x + 1)] = (x2 + 1)(x + 1)(x − 1)2

(iv) x3 − 27, (x − 3)2, x2 − 9

x3 − 27 = (x − 3)(x2 + 3x + 9)

(x − 3)2 = (x − 3)2

x2 − 9 = (x + 3)(x − 3)

Therefore, LCM [(x3 − 27), (x − 3)2, (x2 − 9)] = (x − 3)2(x + 3)(x2 + 3x + 9)
