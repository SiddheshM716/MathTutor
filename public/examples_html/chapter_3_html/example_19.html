Find the square root of the following expressions:

(i) \( 256(x - a)^8 (x - b)^4 (x - c)^{16} (x - d)^{20} \)

(ii) \( \frac{144 \, a^8 \, b^{12} \, c^{16}}{81 \, f^{12} \, g^4 \, h^{14}} \)

1

For part (i), separate the constant and variable terms inside the square root.

2

Use the property \( \sqrt{A \cdot B} = \sqrt{A} \cdot \sqrt{B} \) and \( \sqrt{x^n} = |x^{n/2}| \).

3

Apply the square root to each component:

- \( \sqrt{256} = 16 \)

- \( \sqrt{(x - a)^8} = |(x - a)^4| \)

- \( \sqrt{(x - b)^4} = |(x - b)^2| \)

- \( \sqrt{(x - c)^{16}} = |(x - c)^8| \)

- \( \sqrt{(x - d)^{20}} = |(x - d)^{10}| \)

\( 16 |(x - a)^4 (x - b)^2 (x - c)^8 (x - d)^{10}| \)

Step 1: For part (ii), separate the numerator and denominator inside the square root.

Step 2: Use the property \( \sqrt{\frac{A}{B}} = \frac{\sqrt{A}}{\sqrt{B}} \) and \( \sqrt{x^n} = |x^{n/2}| \).

Step 3: Apply the square root to each component:

- \( \sqrt{144} = 12 \), \( \sqrt{81} = 9 \)

- \( \sqrt{a^8} = |a^4| \), \( \sqrt{b^{12}} = |b^6| \), \( \sqrt{c^{16}} = |c^8| \)

- \( \sqrt{f^{12}} = |f^6| \), \( \sqrt{g^4} = |g^2| \), \( \sqrt{h^{14}} = |h^7| \)

\( \frac{4}{3} \left| \frac{a^4b^6c^8}{f^6g^2h^7} \right| \)
