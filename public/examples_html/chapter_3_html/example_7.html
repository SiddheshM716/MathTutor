Example 3.7

Solve the following system of equations:

3x - y = 12 ... (1)

7x - 2z = 42 ... (2)

2y + 3z = 78 ... (3)

Eliminating z from (2) and (3):

(2) × 3 ⇒ 21x - 6z = 126

(3) × 2 ⇒ 4y + 6z = 156

Adding these equations: 21x + 4y = 282

(1) × 4 ⇒ 12x - 4y = 48

Adding these equations: 33x = 330 ⇒ x = 10

Substituting x = 10 in (1): 30 - y = 12 ⇒ y = 18

Substituting x = 10 in (2): 70 - 2z = 42 ⇒ z = 14

Therefore, x = 10, y = 18, z = 14.
