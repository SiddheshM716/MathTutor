If \( A = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} \), \( B = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 3 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \), find \( A + B \).

1

Identify the given matrices \( A \) and \( B \).

2

The formula for matrix addition is to add corresponding elements of the matrices.

3

Perform the addition element-wise:

\[ A + B = \begin{bmatrix} 4 & 5 & 6 \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 3 & 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 4+1 & 5+3 & 6+1 \end{bmatrix} \]
