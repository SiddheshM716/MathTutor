Find the value of \( a, b, c, d \) from the following matrix equation.

\[

\begin{bmatrix}

d & 8 \\

3 & a

\end{bmatrix}

+

\begin{bmatrix}

2 & 2a \\

1 & 0

\end{bmatrix}

=

\begin{bmatrix}

3b & a \\

-2 & -4

\end{bmatrix}

+

\begin{bmatrix}

b & 4c \\

-5 & 0

\end{bmatrix}

\]

1

Add the matrices on both sides of the equation.

\[

\begin{bmatrix}

d+2 & 8+2a \\

3+1 & a+0

\end{bmatrix}

=

\begin{bmatrix}

3b+b & a+4c \\

-2-5 & -4+0

\end{bmatrix}

\]

\[

\]
